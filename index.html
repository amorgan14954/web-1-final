About

Anna Morgan is an award-winning graphic designer who communicates culturally appropriate messages to target audiences using a variety of visual solutions for print, video, and web designs. Ms. Morgan provides expertise in designing outreach and reporting materials concerning issues that affect underserved populations, including suicide prevention, health care, mental and behavioral health, safety, and education.

In addition, Ms. Morgan provides Section 508-compliant document remediation that follows the U.S. government guidelines in formats universally accessible to individuals who use assistive technologies and uses an assortment of tools and methods to guarantee all designs are visually clear and accessible to anyone who may have visual disabilities.
